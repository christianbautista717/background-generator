If you haven't done so already, it is time to contribute to your first open source project! The contribution can be as big as a new feature, to as small us fixing a typo in the README.md file. Here is what we are going to do:


1. Join our slack community and make sure you are in the #group-project channel. 

2. Read the git-started-guide which is attached to this lecture as extra resource. It is a PDF file that will show you exactly what to do thanks to one of our fellow students @Wan (Make sure you message her on slack to thank her for this beautiful masterpiece)

3. Contribute ONE Pull Request to open source projects in our new community: https://github.com/zero-to-mastery

4. Share your pull request on the slack channel #group-project so others can see your awesome contribution!
